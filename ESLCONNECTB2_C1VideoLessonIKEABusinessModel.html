Thought-Provoking Questions

1. Is IKEA’s maze-like store layout clever or manipulative?

2. Do people value things more when they assemble them?

3. Is flat packing good for customers, companies, or both?

4. Should low price be more important than convenience?

5. Why do IKEA products appeal to different income groups?

6. What other companies use customer participation as part of their model?

7. How could IKEA improve the shopping experience without losing sales?
