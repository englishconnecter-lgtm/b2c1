B2/C1 Grammar: Passive Voice for Compliance

This section practises the passive voice for describing rules, procedures and investigations in financial crime prevention.

Useful language: transactions are monitored / deposits may be flagged / records are reviewed / suspicious activity must be reported

Grammar Section 1: Choose the Correct Passive Form

Choose an answer to get instant green/red feedback.

1. Large deposits ___ by bank officials.

2. Suspicious activity ___ to the compliance team.

3. The source of funds ___ during an investigation.

Grammar Section 3: Useful Patterns

PatternExampleUse
is/are + past participleTransactions are reviewed.Describe a standard process.
may be + past participleDeposits may be flagged.Describe possibility.
must be + past participleSuspicious activity must be reported.Describe obligation.
can be + past participleRisk can be reduced.Describe what is possible.
was/were + past participleThe account was investigated.Describe a past investigation.
Task: Explain how a bank prevents money laundering. Use at least five passive forms.
