Lesson Aim

Students watch a video about OPod, an experimental micro-housing design in Hong Kong, and discuss affordable housing, small-space design, recycled materials and urban innovation.

Before watching

  1. What makes housing expensive in big cities?
  2. Could you live in a 15-square-metre home?
  3. What makes a tiny home practical?

Thought-Provoking Questions

1. Is micro-housing a realistic solution to housing crises?

2. What is the smallest home you could live in comfortably?

3. Should cities use spare land for temporary housing?

4. What are the risks of designing housing mainly for young people?

5. Can recycled construction materials be attractive?

6. Would you prefer a small central home or a larger home far away?

7. What other urban problems need bold design solutions?
