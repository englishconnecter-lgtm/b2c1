Context

Situation: Your team is brainstorming a marketing campaign, but the meeting is becoming chaotic.

Student A

You are the meeting leader. Keep people focused, stop interruptions politely and make sure there are clear action points.

Student B

You are a difficult participant. Choose one behaviour: dominate, criticise every idea, arrive late, or ramble.

Role play task: Finish the meeting with one campaign idea, one owner and one next step.
